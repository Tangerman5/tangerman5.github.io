Initial Planning Document

Site Purpose Statement

The purpose for this site is simply to give the user a very simple, sophisticated, easy to use website. It is mostly geared towards someone who would want to know the simple things about the weather, such as the high and low for the day, wind, and other noticeable factors. It would not, however, be geared towards someone who wants to plan out their week's activities, as there is no kind of forecasting whatsoever. On a mobile device, it shows you right off the bat everything you would need to know for the day.

Audience

The audience with this website could really be anyone. If someone wants to plan out what to wear for the day, to determine whether or not it would be a good day to play outdoor sports, or even to go to the beach, this webiste is for them! I've checked the weather for many occasions and I know many others who similarly check the weather for small things. Due to the planning of the website, it would be easy to use on a computer, on the go with a phone, or even in the comfort of your living room on a tablet.

Persona

Spencer is a 24 year old atmospheric science major. He is about to start grad school at San Jose State to continue into their master's program. He often plans trips based off of the weather, and has always done so for the family since he was 8. He is an outdoorsman who plans backpacking trips in Idaho every summer, and needs to plan what to bring based off the weather. He always has at least one tab on his browser dedicated to the weather so he can access it anytime he is using his computer.
